Distributing Loyalty Points via Website Loyalty Management Extension

The Odoo app introduces three new loyalty points award basis to the customers which you need to define from the backend. These three award bases are
Product Category: This award lets you assign loyalty points to each product category and choose it from the loyalty management configuration setting.
Product Template: The method permits you to use loyalty points configured in the product template to be allotted to customers upon purchase of the product.
Product Variants: It allows you to configure loyalty points in product variants which will be added to sales orders upon selecting that product variant.

Need for Website Loyalty Management Extension

Odoo Website Loyalty Management Extension compliment your Loyalty management by enhancing its functionalities. This Odoo app adds multiple loyalty award basis in Odoo so you can choose the desired one for allocating loyalty points. The added award methods enable you to define loyalty points for each product category, variant, and template.
The module lets you add custom loyalty points for each added loyalty award base which encourages customers to stay loyal to your business/brand. These loyalty points keep customers engaged and less likely to switch to competitors.
Loyalty reward points increase revenue as loyal customers tend to make repetitive purchases, spend more per transaction, and refer your business to others.

Configuring the Loyalty Reward Type

  • The extension adds three new loyalty award basis to the base Loyalty Management module.
  • You can allot loyalty points based on product category, template, and variant.

Defining Loyalty Points for Each Type

  • You can assign different loyalty points for each of the added loyalty award basis.
  • You can add the desired number of loyalty points for each of the types.

Assigning Loyalty Points on Order

  • You can grant Loyalty points to customers for the order placed from the backend and the Odoo website.

Redeeming Loyalty Points

  • The loyalty points can be redeemed from the Odoo website only.
